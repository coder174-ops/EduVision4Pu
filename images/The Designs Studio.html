About us

THE DESIGNS STUDIO is a multifaceted 3D Rendering company. We utilize our talents in the broad field of 3D rendering to create highly realistic architectural renders, 3D animations, product renders and just about everything in between.

We employ a small team of passionate 3D Artists who love architecture and the creation of computer-generated images and animations. We live to create stunning images that help our clients communicate their vision to the regulatory board, investors, and buyers
